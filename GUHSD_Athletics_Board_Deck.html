Recommended First Action

Approve the Athletic Trainer restructuring tonight.
Address the remaining items in subsequent meetings.

  • The cost: $765,000 ongoing + $12,670 one-time = 0.24% of general fund; 1.7–2.6% of reserves
  • The safety case: Pierpoint 2018 — 6× higher recurrent injury rate at schools without ATs (directly applies to our 4 walk-on sites)
  • The Title IX case: Morgan Hill OCR precedent requires equitable AT access regardless of booster funding source
  • The peer case: 6 of 7 SD County peers employ AT as a classified healthcare role, including districts spending up to 28% less per ADA than GUHSD
  • The proposal is district-internal: The athletics department has already drafted a hybrid model with Advantage Athletic Training Company for the 4 walk-on sites and CTE retention at the 5 internal sites
  • Funding headroom: $30M–$45M reserves exist specifically to fund strategic obligations. This is one.
