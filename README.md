## The headline

GUHSD has the 3rd-highest per-ADA spending in San Diego County ($21,644/ADA per CDE FY 2023–24) and holds general fund reserves ~4× the state-mandated minimum. Yet:

- **Both GUHSD conversion charters fund ~40% more coaching stipends** per school than GUHSD does (Helix 73, Steele Canyon 72, GUHSD 52)
- **Helix spends ~2× per school** on athletic stipends ($693,148 vs. ~$349K)
- **GUHSD's Athletic Trainer is a $22,500 teacher stipend** — 6 of 7 SD County peers employ AT as a classified healthcare role with benefits and BOC certification required
- **Granite Hills families and ASBs are fundraising ~62% of officials costs** ($35K of a $57K per-school spend)

The athletics department has drafted a fully-costed $1.4M proposal package. That's 0.44% of the general fund and 3–5% of reserves.
