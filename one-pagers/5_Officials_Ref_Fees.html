The 38% District Share
GUHSD Board Brief Granite Hills Athletics
April 2026
At Granite Hills, actual FY 2025–26 competition overhead totaled $57,393. The district allocation covered $22,000 (38%). The remaining $35,393 was absorbed by sport-specific trust accounts funded through ASB and family booster fundraising. Scaled across 9 GUHSD comprehensive schools, the district-wide ASB burden is approximately $162K–$297K per year.

Why the gap keeps widening

  • CHSSO fees rise annually; GUHSD's $22K per-school allocation has been static — not indexed to CHSSO inflation. Each year the allocation covers a smaller share of actual cost.
  • Sub-varsity contests, tournament fees, and transportation (CHSSO Appendix 1) are not visible in the $22K allocation but are mandatory school-paid line items.
The Ask · Move the per-school allocation toward actual cost
Raise the per-school officials allocation from $22K → $40K–$50K with automatic annual indexing tied to CHSSO rate increases. Even a $40K allocation would close roughly half of the current ASB burden at the largest sites; a $50K allocation reduces the booster/family burden to roughly 15%.
Cost: 9 schools × ($40K – $22K) = $162K/year (~0.05% of $321M general fund); or 9 × ($50K – $22K) = $252K/year (0.08% of GF). Internal proposal currently estimates the increase at $27K–$72K; the higher target ($40K–$50K) brings the allocation closer to verified Granite Hills actuals.
