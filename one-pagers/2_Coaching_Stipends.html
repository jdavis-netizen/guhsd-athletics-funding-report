Issue 2 of 6 · Coaching Stipends
Three Peers Pay More
GUHSD Board Brief Granite Hills Athletics
April 2026
Three SD County peers — Helix, San Marcos, and Steele Canyon Charter — pay head varsity football coaches more than GUHSD at every experience tier. GUHSD's stipend cap of 52 positions per school is materially below the conversion charters' allocations (Helix 73, Steele Canyon 72).

The Ask · From GUHSD athletics' own costed proposal
(1) Eliminate the 25% "head coach without assistants" pay-equity gap district-wide. (2) Add named assistant stipend lines for high-staff sports (mirror Sweetwater / SMUSD). (3) Reclassify the AD off the JV/Assistant schedule line and onto a weighted administrative tier consistent with peers.
Cost: $132,957 (head-coach equity) + $96,714 (additional discretionary stipends) + $71,730 (AD alignment to ~$12K SDUSD benchmark) = ~$301K/year — under 0.1% of the $321M general fund.
