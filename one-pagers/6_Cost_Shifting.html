The Coaches the District Won't Pay
GUHSD Board Brief Granite Hills Athletics
April 2026
Granite Hills is paying $96,009 out of student trust accounts to compensate 53 coaches the district won't put on stipend — formally authorized via the GUHSD "Request To Pay Coach – Trust" Google Form, routed through district HR via PAF, but funded entirely by ASB. GUHSD allocates 52 stipend slots ($313,200), split across 58 coach positions to stretch coverage (e.g., one Frosh football stipend split 4 ways). Add $27,710 in non-league officials, clearance, and ancillary fees separately invoiced to ASB and the verified FY 2025-26 cost-shift hits $123,719+.

What this changes about the funding-gap argument

  • 52 district stipends — split across 58 coach positions to stretch coverage — still aren't enough for a 6A program. Head coaches use the formal Trust Pay form to authorize 53 ADDITIONAL coaches at $96,009 from booster trusts — routed through district HR via PAF, funded entirely by ASB.
  • Adding trust coach pay ($96,009) + non-league officials/clearance/etc. ($27,710) to the prior $35,393 "officials gap" brings Granite Hills booster burden to ~$159,000+ for FY 2025-26 — roughly 4.5× the previously-stated figure. Scaled district-wide: $1.0M–$1.4M+ in district-to-ASB cost-shifting.
  • Granite Hills' $20K AD Athletic Needs Fund (#5032-06) is exhausted — $26,391 committed by 4/29 against $20K budget; covered routine equipment, tournament fees, and 3 GUHSD "Athletic Needs" invoices.
The Ask · Stop the cost-shifting
(1) Move the 53 booster-paid coaches INTO the district payroll — they're already going through district HR PAFs, just funded from the wrong source. (2) Stop billing ASB for Coach Clearance ($70 × ~80 coaches district-wide = $5,600/yr). (3) Roll Non-League Officials into the officials allocation increase (already in the $1.4M proposal). Net: makes the booster-relief case ~4× stronger than officials-only framing.
